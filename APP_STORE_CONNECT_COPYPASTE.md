## Snags That Usually Block Submission

- Paid Apps agreement, tax, or banking not fully active.
- IAP/subscription not set to Ready to Submit.
- IAPs not attached on the app version submission page.
- Storefront mismatch (app available where IAP is not available).
- Screenshot family/size mismatch.
- Metadata claims not matching actual paywall/features/prices.
- Missing/weak App Review notes for HealthKit + purchase flow.
- Wrong sandbox tester account used during QA.
